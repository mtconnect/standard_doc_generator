\subsection{Components} \label{sec:Components}


\block{Components} groups information describing physical parts or logical functions of a piece of equipment.   \block{Components} \glspl{organize} one or more \block{Component} elements.

\subsubsection{Component}
\label{sec:Component}



A \block{Component} organizes information describing a physical part or logical function of a piece of equipment.

Descriptions for attributes of \block{Component}:

\begin{itemize}

\item \property{id}[Component] : The unique identifier for this element.

\item \property{name}[Component] : The name of an element or a piece of equipment.

\item \property{nativeName}[Component] : The common name normally associated with a piece of equipment or an element.

\item \property{sampleInterval}[Component] : An optional attribute that is an indication provided by a piece of equipment describing the interval in milliseconds between the completion of the reading of the data associated with the \block{Device} element until the beginning of the next sampling of that data.

\item \property{sampleRate}[Component] : \textbf{DEPRECATED} in \textit{MTConnect Version 1.2}. Replaced by \property{sampleInterval}[Component].

\item \property{uuid}[Component] : The unique identifier for an element.
\end{itemize}

Descriptions for attributes of \block{Description}:

\begin{itemize}

\item \property{manufacturer}[Description] : The name of the manufacturer of the physical or logical part of a piece of equipment represented by this element.

\item \property{model}[Description] : The model description of the physical part or logical function of a piece of equipment represented by this element.

\item \property{serialNumber}[Description] : The serial number associated with a piece of equipment.

\item \property{station}[Description] : The station where the physical part or logical function of a piece of equipment is located when it is part of a manufacturing unit or cell with multiple stations.

\item \property{value}[Description] : The description of the element.
\end{itemize}
