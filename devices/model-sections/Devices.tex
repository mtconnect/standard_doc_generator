\subsubsection{Device}
\label{sec:Device}



A \block{Device} is a \block{Component} that represents a piece of equipment that produces \glspl{observation} about itself. It \glspl{organize} its parts as \block{Components}.

A \block{Device} \textbf{MUST} have a \property{name} and \property{uuid} attribute to identify itself. 

A \block{Device} \textbf{MUST} have the following \block{DataItems}: \block{Availability}, \block{AssetChanged}, and \block{AssetRemoved}.

See \sect{Component} for details on the \block{Device} model.
