\subsubsection{Device}
  \label{sec:Device}



A \block{Device} is a \block{Component} that represents a piece of equipment that produces \glspl{observation} about itself. It \glspl{organize} its parts as \block{Components}.

A \block{Device} \textbf{MUST} have a \property{name} and \block{uuid} attribute to identify itself. 

A \block{Device} \textbf{MUST} have the following \block{DataItems}: \block{AVAILABILITY}, \block{ASSET}, and \block{ASSET}.

See \ref{Component} for details on the \block{Device} model.
