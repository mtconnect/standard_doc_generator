An Asset&#160;is something that is used in the manufacturing process, but is not permanently associated with a single piece of equipment, can be removed from the piece of equipment without compromising its function, and can be associated with other pieces of equipment during its lifecycle.</body>

\begin{itemize}

\item \property{deviceUuid}[Asset] : The piece of equipment's uuid that supplied the Asset's data.
\item \property{removed}[Asset] : An indicator that the Asset has been removed from the piece of equipment.
\item \property{timestamp}[Asset] : The point in time time the Asset data was last modified.
